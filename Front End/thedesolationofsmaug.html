The Desolation of Smaug

From Tolkien Gateway

Overview

The Desolation of Smaug is the second part of the Hobbit film trilogy, directed by Peter Jackson. The story continues the journey of Bilbo Baggins and the company of dwarves as they face new challenges and confront the dragon Smaug.

Plot

Bilbo and the dwarves continue their quest to reclaim the Lonely Mountain and its treasure from the dragon Smaug. Along the way, they encounter numerous dangers, including giant spiders, hostile elves, and the treacherous Laketown. Bilbo uses his wits and the One Ring to help the company navigate these challenges and finally confronts Smaug in his lair.

Main Characters

  • Bilbo Baggins: A hobbit who becomes an unlikely hero.
  • Thorin Oakenshield: The leader of the dwarves, determined to reclaim his homeland.
  • Gandalf the Grey: A wizard who guides and aids the company.
  • Balin: A wise and noble dwarf, close friend of Thorin.
  • Kili and Fili: Thorin's nephews and brave warriors.
  • Smaug: The fearsome dragon who guards the treasure of Erebor.
  • Thranduil: The Elvenking, who has a complicated relationship with the dwarves.
  • Bard the Bowman: A skilled archer and defender of Laketown.

Key Themes

  • The adventure and the unexpected journey
  • The discovery of courage and inner strength
  • The value of friendship and loyalty
  • The corrupting influence of power and greed
