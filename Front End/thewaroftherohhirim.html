The War of the Rohirrim

From Tolkien Gateway

Overview

The War of the Rohirrim is an upcoming anime film that explores the history of Rohan and its people. Set in the world of Middle-earth, this film brings to life the epic battles and stories of the Rohirrim.

Plot

The film focuses on the history and legends of the Rohirrim, the horse-lords of Rohan. It delves into their culture, battles, and the significant events that shaped their history.

Main Characters

  • Helm Hammerhand: The legendary King of Rohan who defended his people during the Long Winter.
  • Freca: A powerful lord who sought to usurp Helm's throne.
  • Hama: Helm's loyal servant and warrior.

Key Themes

  • Heroism and bravery
  • The importance of heritage and legacy
  • The bonds of loyalty and friendship
  • The struggle for survival against overwhelming odds

Release Information

  • Developer: New Line Cinema and Warner Bros. Animation
  • Release Date: 2024
  • Platforms: Theatrical release
