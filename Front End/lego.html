LEGO The Lord of the Rings

From Tolkien Gateway

Overview

LEGO The Lord of the Rings is an action-adventure game developed by Traveller's Tales. Based on the film adaptations of J.R.R. Tolkien's "The Lord of the Rings" trilogy, the game features LEGO-styled characters and environments, offering a humorous take on the epic story.

Gameplay

Players can control various characters from the trilogy, each with unique abilities. The game features puzzle-solving, combat, and exploration elements, all set within iconic locations from the films. Players can also collect and use various items to progress through the game.

Key Features

  • Experience the story of "The Lord of the Rings" in a light-hearted LEGO style.
  • Play as over 80 different characters from the trilogy.
  • Solve puzzles, engage in combat, and explore the world of Middle-earth.
  • Collect items and build structures to progress through the game.

Release Information

  • Developer: Traveller's Tales
  • Publisher: Warner Bros. Interactive Entertainment
  • Release Date: November 13, 2012
  • Platforms: PlayStation 3, Xbox 360, Microsoft Windows, Wii, Nintendo DS, Nintendo 3DS, PlayStation Vita
