An Unexpected Journey

From Tolkien Gateway

Overview

An Unexpected Journey is the first part of the Hobbit film trilogy, directed by Peter Jackson. The story follows Bilbo Baggins as he joins a company of dwarves on a quest to reclaim their homeland from the dragon Smaug.

Plot

Bilbo Baggins, a hobbit of the Shire, is recruited by the wizard Gandalf to join a company of thirteen dwarves led by Thorin Oakenshield. Their quest is to reclaim the Lonely Mountain and its treasure from the dragon Smaug. Along the way, Bilbo discovers his courage and wit, and finds a mysterious ring that will shape the fate of Middle-earth.

Main Characters

  • Bilbo Baggins: A hobbit who becomes an unlikely hero.
  • Thorin Oakenshield: The leader of the dwarves, determined to reclaim his homeland.
  • Gandalf the Grey: A wizard who guides and aids the company.
  • Balin: A wise and noble dwarf, close friend of Thorin.
  • Kili and Fili: Thorin's nephews and brave warriors.
  • Smaug: The fearsome dragon who guards the treasure of Erebor.
  • Gollum: A creature corrupted by the One Ring, which Bilbo finds.

Key Themes

  • The adventure and the unexpected journey
  • The discovery of courage and inner strength
  • The value of friendship and loyalty
  • The corrupting influence of power and greed
