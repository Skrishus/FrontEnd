Shadow of Mordor

From Tolkien Gateway

Overview

Middle-earth: Shadow of Mordor is an action-adventure game set in the world of J.R.R. Tolkien's Middle-earth. Developed by Monolith Productions, the game follows Talion, a ranger of Gondor, as he seeks revenge against the forces of Sauron.

Gameplay

The game features an open world with a dynamic weather system and day-night cycle. Players can engage in combat, use stealth to eliminate enemies, and explore various regions of Mordor. The Nemesis System creates unique enemies that remember their encounters with the player.

Key Features

  • Engage in dynamic combat with a variety of weapons and abilities.
  • Use stealth tactics to infiltrate enemy strongholds and eliminate targets.
  • Interact with the Nemesis System to create personalized enemy encounters.
  • Explore the dark and dangerous regions of Mordor.

Release Information
