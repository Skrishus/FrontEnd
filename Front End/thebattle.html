The Battle for Middle-earth

From Tolkien Gateway

Overview

The Battle for Middle-earth is a real-time strategy game developed by Electronic Arts, based on the film adaptations of J.R.R. Tolkien's Middle-earth. Players can command the forces of good or evil in epic battles across the lands of Middle-earth.

Gameplay

Players can choose to play as one of the four factions: Rohan, Gondor, Isengard, or Mordor. Each faction has unique units, buildings, and abilities. The game features a single-player campaign that follows the storylines of the films, as well as a multiplayer mode where players can battle each other online.

Key Features

  • Control heroes and units from the films, including Aragorn, Legolas, Gandalf, and Sauron.
  • Engage in large-scale battles with hundreds of units on screen.
  • Build and upgrade bases to defend against enemy attacks.
  • Experience the epic battles of Middle-earth with high-quality graphics and sound.
